Who are we?

Airdox is a leading provider of air conditioning (aircon) services, specializing in the installation, maintenance, and repair of HVAC systems. With a commitment to delivering top-notch services, Airdox caters to both residential and commercial clients, offering tailored solutions to ensure efficient climate control systems. The company prides itself on its skilled workforce, cutting-edge technology, and customer-centric approach, making it a trusted name in the HVAC industry.

Our Key Services

Air Conditioning Installation:

Professional installation of residential and commercial air conditioning systems.Selection of energy-efficient and eco-friendly aircon units suited to client needs.System design and setup, ensuring optimal performance and airflow.

Air Conditioning Maintenance:

Regular maintenance packages to ensure aircon systems operate at peak efficiency.Comprehensive check-ups, including cleaning filters, inspecting refrigerants, and checking for wear and tear.Preventative maintenance to extend the lifespan of air conditioning units.

Air Conditioning Repair:

Expert troubleshooting and repairs for a wide range of aircon brands and models.Quick response to breakdowns, reducing downtime and inconvenience.Repairing components such as compressors, motors, fans, and thermostats.

Commercial HVAC Solutions:

Custom air conditioning systems designed for offices, factories, retail spaces, and other commercial establishments.Installation of advanced, energy-efficient systems that align with business needs.Ongoing maintenance and emergency repair services for commercial clients.

Ductwork & Ventilation Services:

Design, installation, and repair of ductwork systems to ensure efficient air distribution.Ventilation services to maintain indoor air quality and proper airflow.Upgrades to existing systems for better energy efficiency.

Refrigeration:

At Aidox, we also provide expert refrigeration services for both commercial and residential needs. From installation and maintenance to repairs, our skilled technicians ensure your refrigeration systems operate efficiently and reliably. Whether it’s for a business or home, we deliver quality solutions to keep your products and spaces cool, with minimal downtime and maximum performance.
